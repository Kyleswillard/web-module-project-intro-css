Get Involved

As with all parks throughout the country Yosemite is one that needs help. You, your family, and your community can do its part by volunteering. Volunteer Today

From fire prevention education, to being a tour guide, or even a camp monitor you can help ensure that this natural treasure is here for generations to come.

All Volunteers
Please ensure that you bring your own transportation, equipment, and any other medications, clothes, or provisions that you wish to take with you while volunteering at the park. The park has limited resources to assist you.
